So that is why each work block is called a "Pomodoro." The method is simple. First, you pick a task and then: Work for 25 minutes. Take a 5–10 minute break. Repeat steps 1–2 four times. I follow it often and keep a couple of timers at my desk while I'm working (although they are not tomato-shaped.)
